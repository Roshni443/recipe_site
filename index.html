Chocolate Cake

"This Choclate Cake Recipe is a classic. This verson of recipe if fast and Quick and your kids will love it."

DifficultyPreparationCookingTotal Time
Easy10 min25 min35 min

Ingredients

Chocolate: 200 gm

Sugar: 150 gm

Butter: 200 gm

Egg: 4

Flour: 80 gm

Instructions

1

Melt the choclate And the butter together

2

Mix The Sugar, egg, and the flour

3

Mix everything to get a smooth dough

4

Bake in Oven
